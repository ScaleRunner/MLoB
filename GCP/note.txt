0. These notes assume that your network is in your current path, so ./ should consist your lstm.py file.
1. follow instructions up until 'Running locally'. http://liufuyang.github.io/2017/04/02/just-another-tensorflow-beginner-guide-4.html
2. do this: python -m lstm --train-file=processed_train_1000_data.json --job-dir=./tmp/lstm
3. Follow rest of guide, to train with gcloud:
gcloud ml-engine local train --module-name lstm --package-path ./ -- --train-file processed_train_1000_data.json --job-dir ./tmp/lstm
4. Go to tensorboard using: 
python -m tensorflow.tensorboard --logdir=./tmp/lstm/logs --port=8000 --reload_interval=5

(tensorboard at localhost:8000)

5. Run on the cloud using:

(Make sure to change REGION accordingly)

gcloud ml-engine jobs submit training mltest3 --stream-logs --runtime-version 1.4 --job-d
ir gs://mlip-test/mlip-test3 --package-path ./ --module-name lstm --region europe-west1 -- --train-files gs://mlip-test/proc
essed_train_1000_data.json
